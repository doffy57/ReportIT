Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I submit a report?

Simply create an account, click "Submit Report", fill in the details, and submit. You'll get a report ID to track progress.

❓ How long does it take to resolve issues?

Most issues are resolved within 7-14 days depending on the severity and nature of the report.

❓ Is my information safe?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal data with third parties.

❓ Can I remain anonymous?

Yes, you can submit reports anonymously. However, providing contact info helps us follow up with you.

About Us

Learn More About Our Team and Mission

Who We Are

ReportIT is a community-focused platform dedicated to creating safer and more responsive neighborhoods. We provide residents with an easy-to-use system for reporting community issues and emergencies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities by providing a reliable platform where residents can report concerns quickly and efficiently.

About ReportIT

How ReportIT Works and What We Do

What is ReportIT?

ReportIT is a community-driven reporting platform designed to help residents report non-emergency issues and emergency situations within their local area.

Easy Reporting

Submit reports with a few taps. Attach photos, describe the issue, and let the proper authorities handle the rest.

Real-Time Tracking

Track the status of submitted reports in real time. Users receive updates as their reports are reviewed, processed, and resolved.

Community Impact

By using ReportIT, residents actively participate in improving their community. Every report contributes to faster action and a safer environment.

Safety Tips

During Fire

Evacuate immediately, stay low to avoid smoke, call 911, and never use elevators.

During Flooding

Move to higher ground, avoid walking through flowing water, and don't drive through flooded areas.

During Crime

Go to a safe location, call police, describe the situation clearly, and cooperate with authorities.

Medical Emergency

Call for help immediately, perform CPR if trained, provide first aid, and stay calm.
